Web Developer
-------------

My name is Vladimir, and I am a 16‑year‑old passionate programmer with a growing portfolio of digital projects. From an early age, I’ve been fascinated by technology and the power of code to bring ideas to life — and over the past few years, I’ve turned that fascination into a serious, hands‑on skill set.

I specialize in web development and have gained professional experience working with key programming languages and technologies, including Python, HTML, and JavaScript. Currently, I’m expanding my expertise by actively learning React, a modern JavaScript library that allows for building dynamic and responsive user interfaces.

My coding journey began in 2022, when I first dived into Python. What drew me to it was its elegant syntax and remarkable simplicity — qualities that made learning intuitive and enjoyable. I was quickly captivated by how efficiently Python could solve real‑world problems. By the beginning of 2023, I had progressed to the point where I was confidently developing full projects entirely in Python, from data scripts to small web applications.

In late 2023, my interests naturally expanded toward front‑end development, and I began mastering HTML — the backbone of every web page. This opened my eyes to the world of web design and user experience. Then, in early 2024, I took the next step by learning JavaScript, which brought interactivity and life to my static pages.

By the end of 2024, I had successfully combined these three core technologies — Python, HTML, and JavaScript — to build fully functional, interactive web projects. I’m proud of how far I’ve come in such a short time, and I’m excited to keep growing as a developer.

Beyond programming, I also possess practical skills in video editing and web design, where I use Figma to craft clean, user‑friendly interfaces. These creative abilities complement my technical work and help me approach projects with a holistic, design‑minded perspective.

I’m constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to learn, and I look forward to contributing my skills and enthusiasm to meaningful projects in the future.

* 🌍  I'm based in Russia, Moscow
